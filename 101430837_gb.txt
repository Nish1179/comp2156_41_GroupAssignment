George Brown College is a renowned institution located in Toronto, Canada, offering a wide range of programs to over 31,000 full-time students and more than 64,500 continuing education registrations annually.
The college provides certificates, diplomas, graduate certificates, apprenticeships, and degrees in various fields such as arts, design, information technology, business, community services, early childhood education, construction and engineering technologies, health sciences, hospitality and culinary arts, and more.
Moreover, George Brown College emphasizes student support services such as tutoring, counselling, accessibility services, financial aid support, entrepreneurship hubs, student clubs, and peer mentoring to ensure students have a fulfilling college experience.
